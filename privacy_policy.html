Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how GlowingPath Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Echo Path mobile game ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Echo Path is designed to be played without requiring any personal account or registration. We collect minimal information:

  • Game Progress Data: Your level progress, lives, and settings are stored locally on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To save your game progress locally on your device
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Echo Path displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID and other information to provide personalized advertisements.

AdMob's data collection and use is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Data Storage

All game data (progress, settings, lives) is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate any servers that store your personal information. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

Echo Path uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

Echo Path is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
